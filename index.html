About the Band

The Rhapsody Rock Orchestra combines the electrifying energy of Queen’s greatest hits with the elegance of a classical string quartet. Backed by powerful piano, bass, and drums, this unique ensemble creates a sound and experience like no other.

We were formed especially for the "Queen of the Castle" festival at Fonmon Castle, the first-ever Queen tribute festival in the UK. It was an unforgettable event, and our performance was met with rave reviews from fans and festival-goers alike.

There’s truly nothing else like Rhapsody Rock Orchestra. By seamlessly blending the iconic music of Queen with the sophistication of a classical string ensemble, we deliver a show that captivates audiences and leaves a lasting impression.

Whether at festivals, concert halls, or private events, Rhapsody Rock Orchestra brings the magic of Queen to life with a bold, creative twist.
